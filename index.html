How to Become a Graphic Designer

Assembly time: Varies
Effectiveness rating: 100% effective

Wanting to be a graphic designer but don't know where to start? Read these steps to prepare yourself in the coming years. Remember that becoming a graphic designer will take time and practice so don't give up!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Computer
  • Adobe Account
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Access to a college near you (optional)
  • A lot of Patience

  1. Take a lot of art and computer related classes in high school and/or college.

    Classes like drawing, painting, graphic design, and coding will help you a lot in starting out as a graphic designer. A lot of higher level classes use techniques from these classes so you will have an easier time learning about design.

  2. Get an associate's degree in graphic design.

    A lot of community colleges offer a degree in graphic design that will only take two years to get. This will help get you jobs or further your education in the field.

    College Graduation

  3. Learn graphic design techniques on your own.

    A great way to learn graphic design tips is on Youtube. You will most likely need to look up tutorials for Photoshop and Illustrator using that website.

  4. Get a bachelor's degree in graphic design.

    This is a step you can take after getting your associate's or just skip the associate's and get the bachelor's degree in graphic design. Getting this degree will get you connections into the design world and help start your career as a designer.

  5. Take more drawing, writing, and business classes after college.

    A lot of companies offer classes or workshops that employees can go take as well as contacts to experts who can teach you more techniques as well. These will help you solidify your knowledge and help you create better designs.
